COMPLICHECK LOGO SETUP
======================

To add your logo to the PDF reports:

1. Save your logo image as: assets/complicheck_logo.png
   
2. Recommended specifications:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - Size: 140 x 80 pixels (or similar aspect ratio)
   - Color: Should work well on white background
   
3. The logo will appear:
   - Bottom-right corner of every page
   - Size: 0.7" wide Ã— 0.4" tall
   
4. After adding the logo, just regenerate the report:
   python3 scripts/generate_enhanced_compliance_report.py \
     --components data/your_components.json \
     --compliance data/your_compliance.json \
     --output reports/your_report.pdf
